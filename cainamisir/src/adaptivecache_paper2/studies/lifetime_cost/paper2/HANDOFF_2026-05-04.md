### Phase 4e: recall round-trip via attention unmask (the headline)
Current state: token-level recall (v1/v2) and KV-level mask (v4) are
**unrelated paths**. The headline experiment for Paper 2 is:

> Recall via KV unmask should be ~free vs re-prefill (the v3 cliff).

Needs:
1. New policy `recall_mode="attmask"`. On recall, instead of clearing the
   memento on the message, call `scheduler.unmask_blocks_for_recall(
   request_id, memento_id)` — a method that already exists on Scheduler
   (line ~1827 in scheduler.py) but has no caller yet.
2. A way for the policy (in studies/) to reach the scheduler (in
   external/memento). Options:
   - Add a thin "post-step hook" to `MementoVLLMModel.chat()` that
     accepts a list of `(request_id, memento_id)` pairs and calls into
     the engine's scheduler before the next chat.
   - Or stash the call in a queue on the engine config and let the
     scheduler drain it at the next `step()`.
3. Wire memento_ids end-to-end. The `auto:<req>:<start>:<end>` ID
   convention from `_auto_memento_id` is already in place. Policy
   needs to track which memento_id corresponds to which message obs.
